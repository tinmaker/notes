代码：

plt.subplots(1, 1)
x= range(100)
y= [i**2 for i in x]

plt.plot(x, y, linewidth = '1', label = "test", color=' coral ', linestyle=':', marker='|')
plt.legend(loc='upper left')
plt.show()
